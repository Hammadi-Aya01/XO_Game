## ğŸ® How to Play

1. Launch the app on your Android device
2. Two players take turns tapping on empty cells on the grid
3. Player 1 uses "X" and Player 2 uses "O"
4. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
5. If all cells are filled with no winner, the game is a draw
